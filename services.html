Most clients see measurable improvements within 30 days. Models reach peak accuracy around week 6-8. I require a 3-month minimum because algorithmic strategies compound over time.
Do you work with small businesses?+
Yes — that's who the Growth plan is designed for. Start with the free audit to see if we're a good fit. The pricing is structured to be accessible for businesses serious about growing.
Can you work alongside our existing team?+
Absolutely. Most clients have in-house teams. I act as the algorithmic intelligence layer — providing data, models, and strategic recommendations your team executes.
What happens when algorithms change?+
This is my core advantage. My models detect algorithm shifts within hours through real-time signal tracking. Your strategy adapts automatically. You stay ahead while competitors scramble.
