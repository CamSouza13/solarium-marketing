FAQ

Common questions.

What does "algorithm-driven" actually mean?+
It means we use data science techniques — regression analysis, machine learning, statistical modeling — to understand how platforms decide what content to show. Instead of guessing what works, we build models that predict it. Then every marketing decision we make is based on those models.
What's included in the free audit?+
We analyze your current content across your active platforms, score it against our algorithm models, compare you to competitors, and identify the top signals you're missing. You'll get a report plus a 30-minute call to walk through it. No cost, no commitment.
How long until we see results?+
Most clients see measurable improvements within 30 days. Our models reach peak accuracy around week 6-8 as they accumulate more data. We require a 3-month minimum because algorithmic strategies compound over time.
Do you work with small businesses?+
Yes. Start with the free audit — it's genuinely free and gives you actionable insights regardless of whether you become a client. If you want ongoing help, our Growth plan is designed for businesses serious about scaling.
Can you work with our existing team?+
Absolutely. Most clients have in-house teams. We act as the algorithmic intelligence layer — providing the data, models, and strategic recommendations your team executes. We also offer workshops to upskill your team.
What happens when algorithms change?+
This is our core advantage. Our models detect algorithm shifts within hours through real-time signal tracking. Your strategy adapts automatically. You stay ahead while competitors scramble.
