Frequently Asked

How do you actually "decode" an algorithm?+
We collect thousands of data points from content performance across each platform, then use statistical modeling techniques — regression analysis, random forests, gradient boosting, and neural networks — to identify which variables have the highest predictive power for content distribution. Think of it like reverse-engineering a recipe by analyzing the ingredients and outcomes.
How long until we see results?+
Most clients see measurable improvements within the first 30 days. Our models typically reach peak accuracy by week 6-8 as they accumulate more data from your specific content. We require a 3-month minimum engagement because algorithmic strategies compound over time.
Do you guarantee specific results?+
We don't guarantee specific numbers because algorithms are controlled by the platforms. What we guarantee is rigorous, data-driven methodology. Our average client sees a 347% reach increase, but your results depend on your niche, content quality, and how closely you follow model recommendations.
What happens when algorithms change?+
This is our core advantage. Our real-time signal tracking detects algorithm shifts within hours. Models are automatically retrained when drift is detected, and your strategy adapts accordingly. Clients on the Radiance and Supernova plans get priority alerts.
Can you work with our existing marketing team?+
Absolutely. Most of our clients have in-house teams. We act as the algorithmic intelligence layer — providing the data, models, and strategic recommendations that your team executes. We also offer training workshops to upskill your team's algorithmic thinking.
What data do you need from us to start?+
We need platform analytics access (read-only), your content calendar history, and any existing performance data. The more historical data available, the faster our models reach accuracy. We handle all the data engineering — you just grant access and we do the rest.
